Treat Dispencer for my Dog: Finished

April 2022 - May 2022

Step into the world of "Teddy's Treats," a pet-friendly innovation that's all about indulgence with a twist! Imagine this: I put on my inventor's hat to craft a marvel that'll have your furry companions jumping for joy.

Imagine a reservoir filled to the brim with irresistible dog treats – the kind that your four-legged pals can't resist. But here's where the fun begins: a tailor-made opening, perfectly sized for each treat's escape. And front and center, the showstopper – a clever motor, ready to spin that opening like a game of treat-time roulette.

With a cheerful buzz, the motor springs to life. And like clockwork, treat by treat takes a delightful tumble through that specially-crafted opening. It's a fusion of precision and anticipation, turning snack time into a playful event.

Ladies and gentlemen, "Teddy's Treats" isn't just a dispenser; it's an experience designed to pamper your furry companions. The Treat Dispencer transforms treat time into an interactive game, a symphony of technology and tail wags that brings joy to your pets' day. Get ready to make your pets' tails wag with delight!
